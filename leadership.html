Officer Roles and Duties

President: Provide overall leadership and direction to the club, preside over meetings, coordinate and oversee club activities, serve as the primary liaison between the club and school administration, foster inclusivity, and represent Phi Alpha Theta in official capacities as needed.

Vice President: Support the President in their duties and responsibilities, assume the role of the President in their absence, assist in organizing and executing club activities, collaborate with committee chairs and members to ensure smooth operation, and contribute to maintaining a positive and enthusiastic club atmosphere.

Secretary: Maintain accurate records of club meetings, activities, and minutes, communicate important club information, announcements, and updates to members and school officials through Remind and Instagram, assist in correspondence with external parties, including other organizations and speakers, keep track of member attendance, and manage club documents and records.

Treasurer: Manage the club's financial matters, including budgeting and fundraising, maintain a record of club expenditures, income, and financial transactions, provide regular financial reports to the club membership and school officials, seek and explore funding opportunities to support club initiatives and historical field trips, and ensure financial transparency and accountability within the organization.
