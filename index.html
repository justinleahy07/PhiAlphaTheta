Welcome to Phi Alpha Theta at Carnegie Vanguard High School! We are a passionate and inclusive community dedicated to fostering a deep appreciation for history among our members.

Our mission is to provide history-related opportunities and academic support to students in history courses. Through engaging activities, competitions, and tutoring, we create an environment where historical knowledge thrives.

Join us on our journey to explore the fascinating world of history and make a positive impact on the educational journey of all involved at Carnegie Vanguard!
